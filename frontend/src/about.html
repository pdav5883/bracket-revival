About bracket-revival

A celebration of the premier friends and family sport picking competition on the US calendar, bracket-revival is the scientifically-proven best way to experience March Madness without the March Sadness of seeing all your Final Four teams wiped out in the first weekend. Before every round, you get to re-do all your picks, so your bracket is never busted!
